	S-3/F-3ASR	Automatic shelf registration	Anytime, available to "Well-Known, Seasoned Issuers (WKSI)", which is defined as a company that exceeded $700m float value anytime in the last 60 days or have issued in the last three years at least $1 billion aggregate amount of non-convertible securities other than common equity, in primary offerings for cash, not exchange.	Will be blank for $ amount (unless contains ATM) and state which types of securities issuable	None-Low	In majority of cases, a company meets the WKSI criteria by exceeding the $700m float value.
